Tonic Inhibition
and Traumatic Brain Injury

The inhibitory neuronal circuit of the hippocampus provides a great electrophysiological basis to evaluate the effects of traumatic brain injury (TBI) in inhibitory balance or a neuronal circuit. I evaluated tonic inhibitory currents and GABA induced inhibitory K+ currents after TBI. Assessing their acute and chronic changes, in the perspective of hyperactivity and hypersynchronization observed after TBI, I described impaired inhibitory imbalance in the hippocampal neuronal network.

A. Parga, A. F. Logsdon, W. A. Banks & C. Ransom (2021). Traumatic brain injury broadly affects GABAergic signaling in dentate gyrus granule cells. eNeuro
